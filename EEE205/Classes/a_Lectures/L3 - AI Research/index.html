Big Data (Hitch Hikers Guide)

[!quote] Big Data

[The guide] says, “is big. Really big. You just won’t believe how vastly, hugely, mindbogglingly big it is. I mean, you may think it’s a long way down the road to the chemist’s, but that’s just peanuts to Big Data.”

  • 79 zettabytes of data were generated in the world in 2021.
  • 180 zettabytes of data will be generated in 2025.
  • GPT-3 is a very large language model with about 175B parameters. It is trained on about 45TB of text data from different datasets.
